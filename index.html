Why You Should Attend

Get ready for an extraordinary experience at the highly anticipated Sangfor EPIC Innovation Summit 2024! This annual event offers unique opportunities for technological Exchange, Partnership, Innovation, and Connection—core values that Sangfor proudly offers to our customers.

This year’s theme - SynergAI: Transform Vision into Reality - unveils a series of groundbreaking AI-infused cybersecurity and cloud computing innovations. These technologies work together seamlessly to create synergies that will secure and drive your business forward, transforming your visions into reality.

Join Sangfor executives and leading IT experts at the Sangfor EPIC Innovation Summit. Explore the latest advancements in cybersecurity and cloud computing. Network and exchange with industry peers. Expect insightful presentations, interactive demos, and inspirational discussions as Sangfor strives to Make Your Digital Transformation Simpler and Secure.

More details will be shared as the event approaches. Stay tuned and mark your calendar for this exciting event!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Sangfor EPIC Summit?

The Sangfor EPIC Summit is Sangfor’s main annual technology summit where we showcase our latest innovations in cybersecurity and cloud computing. EPIC, which stands for Exchange, Partnership, Innovation, and Connection, represents the core values that we offer to our customers and partners.

When is Sangfor EPIC 2024?

The event will take place between 18 and 21 September 2024.
